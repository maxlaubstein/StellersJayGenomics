I wanted to do analyses (like ABBA-BABA testing) that require an outgroup to the Steller's Jay lineages. Naturally, Blue Jay is the sister species and there's a genome available on NCBI. These scripts are used to create a combined vcf of my Steller's Jay data and data from the Blue Jay reference genome. In short, Blue Jay HiFi reads are mapped to the Steller's Jay reference, and I genotype Blue Jay at all variable sites present in the Steller's Jay vcf.
